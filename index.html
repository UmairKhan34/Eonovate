Case Studies

Unlockd

Unlockd Finance is a decentralized finance (DeFi) platform that empowers users to unlock the value of their digital assets through innovative lending and borrowing solutions. It offers seamless access to liquidity without the need to sell holdings, ensuring users retain ownership while maximizing financial flexibility. The platform prioritizes transparency, security, and user-centric design to deliver a trustworthy experience in the evolving DeFi space.

Industry: Decentralized Finance (DeFi)

Tech Stack: Next.Js, React, TypeScript, Ethers.js, IPFS, Metamask, Solidity

myNFT

MyNFT is a sleek and user-friendly platform designed to help users create, showcase, and trade unique digital collectibles with ease. It focuses on providing a smooth onboarding experience for both new and experienced NFT enthusiasts, with intuitive design and clear features that simplify the world of non-fungible tokens. The platform emphasizes community engagement and accessibility, helping to grow the NFT ecosystem organically.

Industry: Non-Fungible Tokens (NFT) / Digital Collectibles

Tech Stack: Next.Js, React, TypeScript, Storybook, Ethers.js, Hardhat,IPFS, Metamask

MonieWorld

MonieWorld is a fast, affordable remittance platform that lets users in the UK send money instantly to Nigeria through multiple payment methods like bank transfers, cards, Apple Pay, and Google Pay.

Industry:Remittance / Fintech

Tech Stack:Java & Spring Boot backend, Google Spanner database, payment processing via PayrNet, with mobile apps on iOS and Android for user access.

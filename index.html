Unlockd

Unlockd is a DeFi platform that enables users to unlock liquidity from their NFTs without selling them. It offers instant, NFT-backed loans, allowing users to maximize asset value while retaining ownership. With a seamless and secure borrowing process, Unlockd is bridging the gap between NFTs and decentralized finance.

MyNFT

MyNFT is a platform that allows users to create, buy, and sell unique digital assets in the form of NFTs. With an easy-to-use interface, it empowers creators and collectors to explore the world of digital ownership, providing a secure space for trading NFTs and showcasing digital art.

ShowGroundsLive

ShowGroundsLive is a premier event management platform for equestrian competitions, offering real-time scoring, scheduling, and competitor management. Designed for riders, trainers, and organizers, it enhances efficiency, transparency, and engagement in the equestrian sports industry.

AAM Express

AAM Express is a user-friendly food delivery app that offers a seamless ordering experience. With a wide variety of local restaurants, it ensures quick and reliable delivery, bringing fresh meals right to your door. Designed for convenience, Aam Express makes ordering food easier than ever.

ANG Service Hub

ANG ServiceHub provides a seamless and efficient service booking experience. With an intuitive interface, users can easily browse services, schedule appointments, and enjoy fast, reliable assistance from trusted providers. Simplifying service booking, one click at a time.
